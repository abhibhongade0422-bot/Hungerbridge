How HungerBridge Works

Three roles. One mission. Zero waste.

🏨

Donors

Hotels, restaurants & wedding halls post surplus meals with a 2-hour countdown. Build reputation with every listing.

  • 📸 Photo + quantity upload
  • ⏱️ Auto-expiry at 2 hours
  • ⭐ Reputation score tracking
  • 📊 Monthly impact reports
🤝

NGOs

Browse real-time listings, claim food slots instantly, and coordinate pickups — all from a single dashboard.

  • 🗺️ Map-based listing view
  • ✅ One-tap claim system
  • 📦 Track active pickups
  • 📈 Meals received analytics
🚴

Volunteers

Accept dispatch requests, navigate to pickup points, and ensure last-mile delivery to shelters on time.

  • 📍 Live dispatch notifications
  • 🗺️ Turn-by-turn routing
  • ✅ Delivery confirmation
  • 🏆 Volunteer leaderboard
